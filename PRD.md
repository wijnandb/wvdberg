# PRD — Website voor W.G.J. van den Berg Totaal Onderhoud

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** February 2026
**Owner:** Wim van den Berg
**Domain:** wvdberg.com (primary), wvdbergtotaalonderhoud.nl (redirect)

---

## 1. Context & Goal

Wim is an allround carpenter operating solo in Delft and the surrounding Zuid-Holland region. He has been running his own business since 2017, with prior experience at a housing corporation (Rijswijk Wonen) and a professional maintenance services company (O.S.D. B.V.). His specialty is **deuren afhangen** — hanging and fitting doors — a precision skill that separates trained carpenters from general handymen.

His current web presence is completely broken: wvdberg.com returns a 404, wvdbergtotaalonderhoud.nl has an SSL failure. He is invisible online despite having a 5-star Google rating. His email is info@wvdberg.com, suggesting wvdberg.com is his intended primary brand.

**The website's single job: turn visitors into people who call or request a quote.**

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary:** Private homeowners in Delft and surrounding areas (Rijswijk, Voorburg, Zoetermeer, Den Haag corridor) who need a reliable, skilled carpenter for specific jobs — fitting doors, placing window frames, maintenance work.

**Secondary:** Small contractors, renovation companies, or housing associations who need a trusted specialist to handle door hanging and finish carpentry on projects — similar to his O.S.D. and Rijswijk Wonen background.

## 3. What the Site Must Do

### Must-haves

- Display his services clearly, with **deuren afhangen** prominently featured as his specialty
- Make it trivially easy to contact him: phone number always visible, simple quote request form
- Show real photos of his work (he had a Fotoalbum on the old site — this content exists)
- Work correctly: valid SSL, fast loading, mobile-friendly
- Be findable on Google for searches like "timmerman Delft", "deuren afhangen Delft", "kozijnen plaatsen Delft"

### Nice-to-have

- A short personal introduction — who Wim is, his background, his approach
- His 5-star Google reviews embedded or referenced
- Service area map or mention of coverage (Delft en omgeving)

### Out of scope

- Webshop
- Blog
- Client portal
- Anything complex or requiring ongoing maintenance by Wim

## 4. Pages & Structure

One-page site (scrollable) is the right format for a solo tradesman. No need for a multi-page site — it keeps maintenance minimal and keeps visitors focused.

### Sections in order

1. **Header** — Logo, name, phone number, "Vraag offerte aan" button. Always visible on scroll.
2. **Hero** — Strong headline positioning him as Delft's allround carpenter with a specialty in deuren afhangen. A striking photo of his work (the staircase/glass interior from his Google Business profile works well here).
3. **Diensten (Services)** — Clear list of what he does: deuren afhangen (featured), kozijnen plaatsen, timmerwerk, onderhoud. Short descriptions, no waffle.
4. **Werkgebied** — Delft en omgeving, brief mention of the region.
5. **Over Wim** — Short, personal, honest. Background, experience, approach.
6. **Foto's** — Gallery of real work.
7. **Reviews** — His 5 Google reviews.
8. **Contact** — Phone, email, simple form. Name, phone/email, brief description of the job. Nothing more.

## 5. Tone & Design

**Tone:** Direct, no-nonsense, professional but personal. He is a craftsman, not a corporation. The copy should sound like a reliable vakman, not a marketing department.

**Design:** Clean, sturdy, uncluttered. Blue from his existing logo (visible in the LinkedIn banner) as the primary colour. Real photos of real work — no stock imagery. Mobile-first since most people searching for a local tradesman are on their phone.

## 6. Technical Requirements

- Hosted on a reliable platform (his old site was on Weebly/TransIP — fine to keep TransIP hosting, but Weebly should be replaced with something more robust: a simple static site or WordPress)
- Valid SSL certificate (his current site fails on this)
- Redirects from wvdbergtotaalonderhoud.nl to wvdberg.com
- Google Business profile linked and consistent (address, phone, hours)
- Basic on-page SEO: title tags, meta descriptions, service + location keywords in Dutch
- Contact form that sends to info@wvdberg.com
- Page load under 3 seconds on mobile

## 7. Success Criteria

The site is successful if Wim starts receiving inbound quote requests from people who found him online. Given he currently has zero working web presence, even modest SEO traction will be a significant improvement. The 5-star Google rating gives him instant credibility the moment someone lands on the page — the site just needs to not get in the way of that.
